About Me

I earned my Ph.D from Stanford Economics in 2016. Between 2016 and 2018, I worked for a Southeast Asian bank in the Chief Economist role. In 2018 I joined HBS as an Assistant Professor in the Entrepreneurial Management Unit. My research is focused on labor markets and online market design. I'm an NBER Affiliate in Labor Studies, and Associate Editor at the Journal of Political Economy.
